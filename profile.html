Profile

I have over eleven years of experience working with individuals, couples/families, and corporate and educational groups and have worked in a variety of settings including community mental health agencies, courts, State agencies, schools, universities, homes, hospitals, and residential treatment facilities.

With my broad range of experience working with a variety of populations and, as an educator of Psychology, Communications, Interpersonal Skills, and Cultural Diversity, I am able to connect with and relate to people from diverse backgrounds.

In addition, my experience with different styles and methods of therapy enable me to offer appropriate and effective approaches to various issues.

I use an integrated Cognitive-Behavioral Therapy approach in her practice, which means that she combines different theory-based techniques to address the unique needs of her clients.

I am also certified as a Clinical Hypnotherapist and use Task-Oriented, Change Models to assist clients overcome PTSD, phobias, anxieties, fears, etc. to affect life-long, positive, healthy behaviors.
